Get To Know Dr Janique

Meet Dr Janique

I studied at the University of Johannesburg, South Africa, which is also accredited under the European Council of Chiropractic.
Nearing the end of my studies I set out to find a mentor that would guide me both professionally and personally.
I am registered with the Allied Health Professions Council of South Africa (AHPCSA) and a member of the Chiropractic Association of South Africa (CASA) and Paediatric Chiropractic South Africa (PCSA).
​ I was referred to a well known Chiropractor in the northern suburbs who showed me different techniques and enhanced my chiropractic profession extensively.

Who am I able to Treat

I am focused on the health and wellness of the Whole Family, from infants to the elderly, athletes and sportsmen.
I specialise in the care for pregnant mothers and children of all ages.
Come in as a family - I would love to have you.

Mission Statement

With passion and compassion, “My Mission” is in helping others through a positive and mental attitude by providing chiropractic and wellness care to all my patients.
Empowering my patients with inspiration and information needed to choose solutions for themselves and their families.
Maintaining the highest of ethics, integrity, and professional standards today, tomorrow, and for generations to come.

Vision Statement

Everyone deserves the highest quality of a pain free healthy life.
My philosophy is to offer my patients impeccable service in a comfortable environment.
Achieving an understanding and knowledge of the chiropractic treatment they receive, how their body functions and maintaining a healthy life.
